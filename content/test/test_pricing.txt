GHL AGENCY PRICING STRATEGIES AND MODELS

This document covers proven pricing strategies for GoHighLevel agencies.

## Recurring Revenue Models

Agencies typically charge between $500-$2000/month for recurring services. The most common packages include:

**Starter Package ($500-$700/month):**
- Basic website setup
- Lead capture forms
- Email automation
- SMS follow-up sequences
- Monthly reporting

**Growth Package ($1000-$1500/month):**
- Everything in Starter
- Advanced automation workflows
- Multi-channel campaigns
- A/B testing capabilities
- Bi-weekly strategy calls

**Elite Package ($2000-$3000/month):**
- Everything in Growth
- Full white-label platform
- Custom integrations
- Priority support
- Weekly optimization

## High-Ticket Positioning

High-ticket positioning can command $25K-$150K retainer fees for:

- Complete business automation buildouts
- Enterprise-level implementations
- Done-for-you campaign management
- Strategic consulting packages
- Revenue share agreements

## Setup Fees

Most agencies charge one-time setup fees ranging from $1,000 to $5,000 depending on complexity:

- Basic setup: $1,000-$2,000
- Medium complexity: $2,000-$3,500
- Enterprise setup: $3,500-$5,000+

## Value-Based Pricing

Instead of hourly rates, focus on value delivered:

- Calculate ROI for the client
- Price based on business impact
- Tie fees to performance metrics
- Create win-win revenue share models

## Niche-Specific Pricing

Different industries can support different price points:

**High-Value Niches:**
- Real estate agencies: $1,500-$3,000/month
- Medical practices: $2,000-$4,000/month
- Legal firms: $2,500-$5,000/month
- Financial services: $3,000-$6,000/month

**Mid-Value Niches:**
- Local service businesses: $700-$1,500/month
- E-commerce stores: $800-$2,000/month
- Consultants/Coaches: $500-$1,500/month

## Pricing Psychology

Key principles for pricing success:

1. Always offer 3 tiers (anchoring effect)
2. Make the middle option most attractive
3. Price ends in 7 or 9 for psychological appeal
4. Bundle services for perceived value
5. Offer annual discounts (10-20% off)

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

- Underpricing your services
- Not charging setup fees
- Hourly billing instead of value-based
- Competing on price instead of value
- Not raising prices regularly

## Retention Strategies

Keep clients paying month after month:

- Deliver consistent results
- Provide monthly reports showing ROI
- Regular check-in calls
- Continuous optimization
- Proactive communication
- Add value beyond the platform

This pricing framework has been proven across hundreds of successful GHL agencies generating $10K-$50K+ monthly recurring revenue.
