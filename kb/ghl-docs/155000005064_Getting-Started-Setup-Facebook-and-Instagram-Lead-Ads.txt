Connect your Facebook and Instagram channels to manage lead generation ads. Capture high-quality leads directly from social media.
Setup Facebook/Instagram Lead Ads
Navigate to Marketing > Ad Manager
Click Activate Ads Manager for $11 / month
Click Pay $11 and Subscribe
Click Connect Facebook
Select Facebook Page
Click Get Started
Click Create Campaign
Fill Campaign details (Step 1)
Select Lead Generation
Create a Lead Form by clicking Create Facebook Form
Fill Form details (type, greeting, mapping, etc)
Click Create Form
Fill more Ad details (primary text, media, etc)
Click Next
Fill Budget and Audience details (Step 2)
Set budget
Set audience
Click Next
Review (Step 3) and click Publish > Publish Ad Now

Facebook Multi Page Lead Ads Integration
Publish An Existing Ad
Navigate to Marketing > Ad Manager
Click the 3-dot action menu to the right of the ad
Click Edit
On Step 3 Review and Publish, click Publish
