Setting up and launching your online course might seem daunting at first, but trust me—it's simpler than you think! With just a few clicks, you'll be on your way to sharing your expertise, building your audience, and even earning some income. Let's dive in step-by-step.

Create a Course
Creating your first course is like laying the foundation for your teaching empire. It’s exciting and easy—let's walk through it together.
Navigate to
Memberships > Courses > Products
.
Click
Create Product > Start from Scratch
.
Click
Start Building
to pick the format that suits your teaching style:
Sprint Course (quick and concise)
Marathon Course (long-form and comprehensive)
Membership (recurring content)
Build your own (completely customized)
Give your course a memorable name.
Click
Create Product
.
Build out your course content from scratch or edit the template.
Click
Save
.
?
Next step:
Explore more ways to enhance your course:
How To Build Courses / Membership Sites
Paid Courses inside Communities
Advanced Course Unlock Options in Communities
Create an Offer
Once your amazing course is ready, creating an offer is like packaging a beautiful gift—enticing and clearly valuable. Here's how to make it irresistible:
Navigate to
Memberships > Courses > Offers
.
Click
Create Offer
.
Fill in your offer details to clearly communicate the value.
Assign your newly created course product to the offer.
Set your desired price.
Click
Create
.
Switch your Offer from Draft to Published and click Save.
Or switch between draft and published directly in the offers list.
Next you can:
Consider bundling offers or creating discount codes to attract more learners.
Sell Offer
Congratulations, your offer is ready to share with the world! The final step is all about connecting with your audience. Let’s quickly generate and share your offer:
Navigate back to
Memberships > Courses > Offers
.
Click the
link
icon to get the link for an offer.
Click
Copy
.
Share it across your channels—social media, emails, or your website.
Next you can:
Explore marketing strategies or launch a campaign to boost your course sales.
