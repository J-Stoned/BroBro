Agency users can now cancel location-level subscriptions directly from their Billing page using a guided 3-step unsubscribe flow. This article walks you through the cancellation options, what to expect at each step, and what's coming next. If you're looking to manage subscriptions quickly without support intervention, this guide is for you.
Steps to Cancel Location Subscriptions
Step 1:
Navigate to Billing
Click on
Settings
from your Agency account.
Click on the
Billing
tab.
Locate
Reselling
under Subscriptions.
For the particular reselling product, Click on the
Show sub-accounts
button.
Step 2:
Choose the Location
Click on the
Three Dots
under
Actions
column for the location you want to cancel the subscription for.
Click on the
x Cancel Subscription
button.
Step 3:
Choose when to Cancel
You’ll be prompted to select one of the following cancellation options:
Cancel at End of Billing Cycle
– Subscription ends after the current billing period (no refunds).
Cancel Immediately
– Subscription ends right away (no refunds or proration).
Select your desired option and click on the
Proceed to cancellation
button.
Step 4:
Review Location and Important Disclaimer
Review the
Location Name
and
ID
.
Review the
Important Disclaimer
.
Click on the
Next
button.
Step 5:
Share a Reason & Cancel
Select a
reason for cancellation
.
Confirm cancellation by typing
DELETE
in the box.
Click on the
Cancel Subscription
button.
