Ready to simplify your transactions? Stripe integration makes accepting credit card payments, subscriptions, and more straightforward and secure. Let's walk through setting it up together!
Connect Stripe
First, head over to your Payments section. This is where all the magic happens—integrating payment gateways to streamline your customer transactions.
Navigate to
Payments > Integrations
. Here, you'll find popular options like Stripe, PayPal, Authorize.net, NMI, Manual Payment Methods (ex: cash on delivery, COD), and Square.
Click
Connect with Stripe
. This will direct you to securely log in or create your Stripe account if you haven't done so already.
Finally,
authorize your Stripe account
. This step ensures everything syncs smoothly and securely. When the integration is finished you can click
Manage
to edit the connection.
Awesome job! Now your payments are ready to roll.
