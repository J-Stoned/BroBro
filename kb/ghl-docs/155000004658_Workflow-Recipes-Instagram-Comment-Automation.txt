Create a workflow from this recipe to get an example of a full IG "comment to get the file" style workflow.
Trigger: fires on any first level comment on the post.
Action: responds to the comment with "hey check your DM" or "thanks for showing interest. please check your DM".
Action: interactive messenger reply to comment via DM "I am excited that you want to start your Personal Finance journey." with buttons "visit our website" or "get the checklist".
Action: interactive messenger reply to DM "here is your checklist".
