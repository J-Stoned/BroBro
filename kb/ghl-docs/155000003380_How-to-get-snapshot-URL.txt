To add a snapshot to your app, follow these instructions.
Step 1
: Head to 'Account Snapshots' in the Agency view, and select the snapshot that you wish to sell via the app marketplace.
Step 2
: Select 'Share Snapshot' from the options and select 'Get Marketplace share link' from the available options.
Step 3
: Paste your snapshot link in the app and launch your app with snapshot!
