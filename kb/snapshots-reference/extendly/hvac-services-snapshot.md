# HVAC Services Snapshot

## Marketplace

## Description
The HVAC Services Snapshot is a comprehensive solution focused on generating leads, nurturing prospects, and retaining clients for heating, ventilation, and air conditioning businesses. This industry-specific snapshot provides complete setup and customization with pre-built workflows, service-specific funnels, and automated communication systems designed to help HVAC contractors streamline operations and grow their customer base.

## Key Features
- **Lead Generation System**: Optimized funnels and forms designed to capture service requests for HVAC installations, repairs, and maintenance
- **Service-Specific Workflows**: Pre-configured automation sequences for emergency service requests, seasonal maintenance reminders, and equipment replacement campaigns
- **Client Retention Tools**: Automated follow-up systems for routine maintenance scheduling, filter replacement reminders, and seasonal tune-up campaigns
- **Complete Account Setup**: Full configuration including agency accounts, Twilio for SMS alerts, Mailgun for email communications, and Google Account connections
- **Professional Marketing Assets**: Pre-built websites, service showcase pages, and promotional templates tailored for HVAC service providers
- **Training & Support**: Hands-on training to master the CRM system with 6 months of FREE ongoing technical support

## Use Cases
- **Emergency Service Dispatch**: Automated lead capture and rapid response workflows for urgent HVAC repair requests with immediate technician notification
- **Seasonal Maintenance Campaigns**: Scheduled automation for spring AC tune-ups and fall heating system checkups to drive recurring revenue
- **Equipment Replacement Sales**: Nurture sequences targeting aging HVAC systems with replacement offers and financing options
- **Multi-Technician Operations**: Centralized scheduling and dispatch management for HVAC companies with multiple service technicians
