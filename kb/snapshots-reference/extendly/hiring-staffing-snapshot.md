# Ultimate Hiring & Staffing Snapshot

## Marketplace
Extendly

## Description
A comprehensive applicant tracking system built within GoHighLevel that transforms the recruitment process by automating the hiring workflow. This snapshot can cut recruitment time in half by streamlining candidate management from application through hiring decision, with automated communication at every stage.

## Key Features
- Complete applicant tracking system with pipeline stages for screening, interviewing, offering, and declining candidates
- Automated email communication for all recruitment stages including interview invitations, job offers, and candidate status updates
- One-way video interview functionality allowing candidates to record and submit answers for asynchronous review before in-person meetings
- Nurture campaigns to keep your job opening top-of-mind for interested candidates who haven't yet applied
- Pre-written email templates for inviting candidates to interviews, requesting additional information, and announcing new job postings
- Automated workflow triggers that advance candidates through hiring stages based on manager actions

## Use Cases
- Recruiters and hiring managers streamlining their workflows by automating the entire recruitment process within GoHighLevel
- Small business owners who need to gather more candidate information and develop stronger insights before conducting interviews

## Pricing
Standard Extendly snapshot pricing with included white-label tech support and onboarding

## Requirements
- GoHighLevel platform access
- One agency license per HighLevel agency (unlimited use in subaccounts within that agency)
- Video recording capabilities for one-way interview feature
