# Dental Practice Snapshot

## Marketplace
Extendly

## Description
The Dental Practice Snapshot is marketed as the most robust and well-thought GoHighLevel snapshot for dental practices. This comprehensive solution provides complete setup and customization for dental offices, combining automated appointment management, patient communication systems, and marketing tools specifically designed for dental professionals to capture leads, nurture patients, and streamline practice operations.

## Key Features
- **Complete CRM Setup**: Full GoHighLevel CRM customization including agency account setup, Twilio and Mailgun account configuration, and Google Account connections
- **Automated Patient Communication**: Pre-built workflows for appointment reminders, follow-ups, review requests, and patient reactivation campaigns
- **Lead Capture & Nurturing**: Dynamic funnels and forms designed to capture new patient inquiries with automated nurturing sequences
- **Marketing Automation**: Email campaigns, online advertising templates, and social media post templates tailored for dental services
- **White-Label Branding**: Complete customization of branding, color schemes, and domain white-labeling for agency use
- **Comprehensive Support**: Hands-on training to learn the CRM system plus 6 months of FREE support access for ongoing questions and technical assistance

## Use Cases
- **New Patient Acquisition**: Deploy lead capture funnels and automated follow-up sequences to convert inquiries into scheduled appointments
- **Patient Retention**: Automated recall systems for regular checkups, cleaning reminders, and patient reactivation campaigns for inactive patients
- **Review Management**: Systematic review request automation to build online reputation and attract new patients through social proof
- **Multi-Location Practices**: Scalable solution for dental groups managing multiple practice locations with centralized marketing and patient communication
