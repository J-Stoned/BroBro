# Ultimate Small Business Snapshot

## Marketplace
Extendly

## Description
A universal pre-built, customizable automation solution designed to help agencies and small businesses quickly set up GoHighLevel systems without extensive manual configuration. This comprehensive snapshot enables users to have everything up and running within hours rather than spending weeks building from scratch, with full automation for any small business sales and marketing needs.

## Key Features
- 99 pre-built workflows ready to automate essential tasks like appointment reminders, lead follow-ups, and unsubscribe management
- 26 pre-built funnels and websites with fully customizable templates designed to engage customers and drive conversions
- 177 pre-designed email and SMS templates plus long-term nurture sequences for sustained customer engagement
- Multi-channel lead capture system that captures leads from Facebook Messenger, landing pages, website chat, and other sources with automatic source tagging
- Complete sales pipeline that tracks leads from initial capture through completion with built-in review and referral campaigns
- Request a call back forms, schedule an appointment features, and comprehensive forms and funnels needed to run a small business

## Use Cases
- Agencies serving multiple industries looking for ready-to-use pipelines and workflows for real estate, agencies, and small businesses
- Service-based businesses requiring comprehensive appointment management and automated client communication systems
- Retail and restaurant owners focused on customer retention and engagement through automated follow-ups and feedback collection
