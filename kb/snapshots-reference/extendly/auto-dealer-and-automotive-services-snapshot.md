# Auto Dealer & Automotive Services Snapshot

## Marketplace
Extendly

## Description
The Auto Dealer & Automotive Services Snapshot is a comprehensive digital marketing automation system designed specifically for auto dealerships and automotive service businesses including auto mechanics, auto repair shops, and automotive detailing services. This snapshot helps automotive businesses move more inventory and book more service appointments by automating lead capture, follow-up, appointment booking, and customer reactivation from a single dashboard, streamlining workflows and enhancing business efficiency.

## Key Features
- **Inventory Marketing Automation**: Automated lead capture and follow-up sequences for vehicle sales with integration for showcasing available inventory
- **Service Appointment Scheduling**: Integrated calendar system for booking maintenance, repairs, and detailing services with automated reminder workflows
- **Customer Reactivation Campaigns**: Systematic follow-up sequences to bring back previous customers for service appointments and vehicle trade-in opportunities
- **Lead Nurturing System**: Multi-touch email and SMS campaigns designed to convert showroom visitors and service inquiries into customers
- **Trade-In & Financing Funnels**: Optimized landing pages and forms for collecting trade-in valuations and financing pre-qualification information
- **Review Management**: Automated review request workflows to build online reputation for both sales and service departments

## Use Cases
- **New Vehicle Sales**: Deploy lead capture funnels for new car inquiries with automated follow-up sequences to schedule test drives and close sales
- **Service Department Growth**: Automated maintenance reminder campaigns based on mileage or time intervals to drive service department revenue
- **Used Car Inventory Management**: Systematic marketing for used vehicle inventory with automated notifications when vehicles matching customer preferences arrive
- **Customer Lifecycle Marketing**: Comprehensive communication strategy from initial purchase through ongoing service needs and eventual vehicle replacement

## Requirements
- Active GoHighLevel account
- Twilio account for SMS appointment reminders and promotional messages
- Mailgun account for email marketing campaigns
- Google Account for service calendar and appointment scheduling
- Integration capabilities with dealer management systems (DMS) and automotive CRM platforms
- White-label domain configuration for dealership branding
- Mobile-responsive design for sales and service staff access
