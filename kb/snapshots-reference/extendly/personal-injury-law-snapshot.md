# Personal Injury Law Snapshot

## Marketplace
Extendly

## Description
The Personal Injury Law Snapshot is a specialized GoHighLevel solution designed for personal injury attorneys and law firms to automate client intake, case management, and lead nurturing. This pre-built setup includes optimized pipelines, automated workflows, and marketing assets specifically tailored for personal injury practices, enabling law firms to capture, qualify, and convert injury claim leads while maintaining professional client communication throughout the legal process.

## Key Features
- **Client Intake Automation**: Streamlined intake forms and automated qualification workflows to efficiently process personal injury case inquiries
- **Case Management Pipelines**: Pre-configured pipeline stages for personal injury cases from initial consultation through settlement or trial
- **Attorney Consultation Scheduling**: Integrated calendar system for booking free case evaluation appointments with automated reminder sequences
- **Lead Nurturing Campaigns**: Email and SMS sequences designed to maintain contact with potential clients during the decision-making process
- **Marketing Funnel Templates**: Professional landing pages and advertising templates specifically designed to attract personal injury leads
- **Automated Follow-Up System**: Multi-touch follow-up sequences to ensure no potential client falls through the cracks during the intake process

## Use Cases
- **Free Consultation Funnel**: Deploy advertising campaigns with dedicated landing pages offering free case evaluations, automatically routing qualified leads to intake specialists
- **Case Status Communication**: Automated updates to keep clients informed about case progress, document requests, and important milestones
- **Referral Source Management**: Track and nurture relationships with medical providers, chiropractors, and other referral sources through automated communication
- **Multi-Attorney Practice Management**: Centralized lead distribution and case assignment system for law firms with multiple personal injury attorneys
