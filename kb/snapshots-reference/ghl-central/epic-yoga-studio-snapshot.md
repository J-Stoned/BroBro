# Epic Yoga Studio Snapshot

## Marketplace
GHL Central

## Description
A foundational template for establishing yoga studio sub-accounts with a comprehensive toolkit to capture, nurture, and close leads. This snapshot includes everything needed to run a successful yoga business including website, forms, calendars, workflows, email campaigns, online ads, and social posts. The system is designed specifically for yoga establishments, with service calendars for various yoga offerings and automated class management.

## Key Features
- Effective Sales Funnel: Drive traffic to claim offers and book appointments in just a few easy steps
- Service Calendar System: Multiple service calendars for different yoga class types plus event calendar for consultations and workshops
- Staff Assignment: Assign specific instructors to services and track equipment needed for each class
- Service-Specific Email Templates: Email campaigns designed around specific services customers want most from yoga establishments
- Templated Website: Customizable website template for yoga studios without an existing web presence

## Use Cases
- Yoga studios needing to automate class scheduling and member management
- Multi-instructor yoga businesses requiring staff assignment and class management
- New yoga establishments needing complete marketing infrastructure including website and booking system
