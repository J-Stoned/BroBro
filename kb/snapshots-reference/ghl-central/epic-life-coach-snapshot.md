# Epic Life Coach Snapshot

## Marketplace
GHL Central

## Description
A foundational template tailored specifically for life coaches with a comprehensive toolkit to capture, nurture, and close coaching clients. This snapshot includes pre-built marketing campaigns, automated booking systems, and complete client management workflows. The solution features discovery call funnels, breakthrough session campaigns, and no-show recovery automation designed specifically for coaching businesses.

## Key Features
- Pre-Built Marketing Campaigns: Discovery Call nurture campaign with personalized automated texts and emails encouraging leads to book free consultations
- Database Activation: SMS-based breakthrough session offers to activate existing database contacts
- Automated Booking Confirmation: Arrange and confirm coaching session bookings through automated text sequences
- No-Show Campaign: Automated follow-up to re-engage individuals who miss scheduled appointments
- Multiple Service Calendars: Service calendars for different coaching offerings plus event calendar for group sessions

## Use Cases
- Life coaches needing to automate discovery call scheduling and client nurturing
- Coaching businesses seeking to reactivate dormant database contacts with breakthrough offers
- New coaches requiring complete marketing infrastructure including proposal templates and ad templates
