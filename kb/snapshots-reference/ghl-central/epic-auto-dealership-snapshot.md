# Epic Auto Dealership Snapshot

## Marketplace
GHL Central

## Description
A comprehensive auto dealership marketing solution featuring multiple funnels, AI-powered automation, and lead conversion tools. This snapshot enables dealerships to craft multiple offers, capture leads, and convert opportunities into paying customers through integrated automations, calendars, and pipelines. The system includes conversational AI to engage prospects and automated booking systems for test drives and consultations.

## Key Features
- Multiple Sales Funnels: Craft multiple offers and capture leads with funnels integrated with automations, calendars, and pipelines
- Conversational AI: ChatGPT engages prospects and AI Booking Bot sets and finalizes appointment details on autopilot
- Video Sales Letters: Included VSL to warm up prospects before sales conversations
- AI-Driven Missed Call Text Back: Capture every lead even during busy dealership hours
- Automated Onboarding: Workflow that simplifies bringing in new customers and post-purchase follow-up

## Use Cases
- Auto dealerships seeking to automate test drive scheduling and lead follow-up
- Multi-location dealership groups requiring consistent marketing and sales processes
- Used car dealerships needing comprehensive lead nurturing for longer sales cycles
