# Epic Mortgage Broker Snapshot

## Marketplace
GHL Central

## Description
A comprehensive mortgage broker toolkit designed to capture, nurture, and close leads through automated workflows and professional marketing assets. This snapshot includes a fully functional 10-page website, multiple calendars, workflows, email campaigns, and proven copywriting created by mortgage marketing experts. The solution offers dynamic branding capabilities allowing easy customization across all touchpoints.

## Key Features
- Fully Functional Website: 10-page mobile-responsive website including home, about, services, blog, testimonials, contact, and policy pages
- Comprehensive Workflows: Six workflows including appointment booking, reminders, cancellation, no-show workflows, social media alerts, and review collection
- Service Calendar System: Multiple service calendars for different loan types plus event calendar for consultations
- Sales Pipeline: Monitor progress of opportunities as leads move through the mortgage process
- Dynamic Copywriting: Update contact and branding information across entire website, emails, and SMS easily with custom values

## Use Cases
- Mortgage brokers and loan officers needing automated lead nurturing and appointment systems
- New mortgage businesses requiring a complete web presence and marketing infrastructure
- Multi-broker firms seeking consistent branding and workflows across all loan officers
