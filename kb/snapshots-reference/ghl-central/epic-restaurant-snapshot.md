# Epic Restaurant Snapshot

## Marketplace
GHL Central

## Description
A complete restaurant marketing and customer engagement solution combining powerful funnels, engaging websites, seamless workflows, and efficient lead-capturing processes. The snapshot integrates advanced AI and GPT technology to provide 24/7 marketing support, ensuring restaurants never miss a customer opportunity even during busy service hours.

## Key Features
- AI-Driven Missed Call Text Back: Capture every lead even during busy moments with automated SMS responses
- Marketing Automation: Extensive workflow automation including lead gen, database reactivation, calendar appointments, and messaging sequences
- Multi-Channel Integration: Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, SMS, and Chatbot integrations for comprehensive customer reach
- AI Booking Bot: ChatGPT engages prospects and AI Booking Bot sets and finalizes reservation details
- Automated Customer Onboarding: Workflow that simplifies bringing on new customers and first-time diners

## Use Cases
- Restaurants seeking to automate reservation management and customer follow-up
- Multi-location restaurant groups requiring consistent marketing across all locations
- New restaurants needing a complete marketing infrastructure including website and automation
