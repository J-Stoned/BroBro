# Dentist Snapshot - Free Whitening Offer

## Marketplace
Official Marketplace

## Description
The Dentist Snapshot is a pre-built marketing template designed specifically for dental practices offering complimentary teeth whitening services tied to appointment scheduling and long-term patient retention campaigns. This snapshot provides a complete promotional framework centered around a "Free Teeth Whitening With Next Appointment + 6 Months Check-in Campaign" strategy.

## Key Features
- **Integrated Campaign Template**: Complete snapshot template configured for rapid deployment of dental marketing campaigns
- **Sales Funnel**: Pre-built funnel specifically designed for the free whitening offer with optimized conversion paths
- **Facebook Advertising Assets**: Example Facebook advertisement creative and copy ready for customization and launch
- **Automated Follow-Up Campaign**: Six-month patient check-in campaign with automated reminders and engagement sequences
- **Setup Documentation**: Comprehensive setup guide with step-by-step instructions for template customization
- **Video Tutorial**: Overview video demonstration showing implementation and best practices
- **Lead Capture Forms**: Pre-configured forms for appointment booking and patient information collection
- **Patient Nurturing Workflows**: Automated sequences for appointment reminders, post-visit follow-ups, and review requests

## Use Cases
- **New Patient Acquisition**: Attract new patients with compelling free whitening offers, converting prospects into scheduled appointments
- **Practice Growth Campaigns**: Launch targeted Facebook advertising campaigns to expand patient base in local markets
- **Patient Retention & Recall**: Implement automated six-month check-in campaigns to maintain regular patient visits and improve retention rates
- **Promotional Marketing**: Quickly deploy time-sensitive promotional offers for dental services tied to appointment scheduling
