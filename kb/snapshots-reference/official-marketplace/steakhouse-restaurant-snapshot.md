# Steakhouse Snapshot

## Marketplace
Official Marketplace

## Description
The Steakhouse Snapshot functions as a foundational template specifically designed for steakhouse restaurant sub-accounts. It provides a complete toolkit for the customer acquisition lifecycle with features tailored to restaurant marketing, reservation management, and customer retention strategies. The snapshot includes a signature "Free Appetizer" promotional campaign integrated with mailing list growth.

## Key Features
- **Restaurant Website Platform**: Full-functional 10-page website including home, about us, menu/services, blog, testimonials, contact, and policy pages with mobile-responsive design
- **Lead Capture Forms**: Customizable forms for mailing list signups, reservation requests, and special event inquiries
- **Reservation Calendar System**: Integrated appointment/reservation calendars for table bookings and event scheduling
- **Multiple Marketing Funnels**: Pre-built funnels for various offers including the signature free appetizer campaign, with integration to automations, calendars, and pipelines
- **Automated Workflows**: Complete workflow sequences for reservation confirmations, appointment reminders, and holiday promotional campaigns
- **Email Marketing Campaigns**: Ready-to-deploy campaigns for customer nurturing, special promotions, and seasonal offerings
- **Facebook Advertising Templates**: Pre-built Facebook ad creative for the free appetizer offer with done-for-you funnel integration
- **Social Media Post Templates**: Pre-configured social media content for restaurant promotion and engagement
- **AI-Driven Features**: Missed call text back functionality to capture every lead during busy service hours
- **Call-to-Action Optimization**: Strategic CTA sections throughout the website designed for lead conversion

## Use Cases
- **New Customer Acquisition**: Launch the free appetizer campaign with Facebook advertising to grow mailing lists and drive reservation bookings
- **Reservation Management**: Streamline table booking processes with automated confirmation messages and reminder sequences
- **Holiday & Event Promotion**: Deploy automated campaigns for special events, holidays, and seasonal promotions with built-in reminder systems
- **Customer Retention**: Implement mailing list nurture campaigns and loyalty programs to encourage repeat visits and increase customer lifetime value
