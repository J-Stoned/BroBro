# Chiropractor Snapshot

## Marketplace
Official Marketplace

## Description
The Chiropractor Snapshot functions as a foundational template designed for establishing sub-accounts within chiropractic practices. It provides a comprehensive toolkit to capture, nurture, and close leads, encompassing multiple integrated tools for practice management and patient marketing specifically tailored to the chiropractic industry.

## Key Features
- **Professional Website Builder**: Done-for-you chiropractor website templates with industry-specific design and content structure
- **New Patient Campaign**: Pre-built campaign specifically designed for new patient appointments with qualifying surveys to assess patient needs
- **Form Creation Tools**: Customizable patient intake forms, consultation request forms, and assessment questionnaires
- **Calendar & Scheduling System**: Integrated booking system for appointments, consultations, and follow-up visits
- **Automated Workflows**: Pre-built workflow automations for patient onboarding, appointment reminders, and retention campaigns
- **Email Campaign Functionality**: Ready-to-deploy email sequences for patient education, wellness tips, and practice updates
- **Social Media Management**: Pre-built social posts and Facebook advertising templates for local practice promotion
- **Online Advertising Capabilities**: Integrated tools for digital advertising campaigns targeting local patients
- **Offer Funnel Template**: Pre-configured funnels for special offers, free consultations, or initial assessment promotions

## Use Cases
- **New Practice Setup**: Complete digital infrastructure for new chiropractic practices, providing immediate professional online presence and patient acquisition tools
- **Patient Acquisition Campaigns**: Launch targeted campaigns for new patient appointments with qualifying surveys and automated follow-up sequences
- **Practice Growth & Marketing**: Implement comprehensive marketing automation including social media, email campaigns, and online advertising for practice expansion
- **Patient Retention Programs**: Automated appointment reminders, wellness check-in campaigns, and re-engagement sequences to improve patient retention and lifetime value
