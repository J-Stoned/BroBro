# Real Estate Agent Snapshot

## Marketplace
Official Marketplace

## Description
The Real Estate Agent Snapshot functions as a foundational template designed to establish sub-accounts for real estate professionals. It provides a comprehensive toolkit to capture, nurture, and close leads through an integrated marketing and customer management system specifically tailored to the real estate industry.

## Key Features
- **Website Builder**: Full-featured website creation tools with real estate-focused designs including 10+ pages (home, about, services, listings, testimonials, contact, and policy pages)
- **Lead Capture Forms**: Customizable forms integrated with lead-capturing workflows for property inquiries and buyer/seller information
- **Calendar & Scheduling**: Service-specific calendars for property viewings, consultations, and estimates, plus event calendars for open houses
- **Automated Workflows**: Pre-built automation sequences for lead nurturing and follow-up throughout the sales funnel
- **Email Marketing Campaigns**: Ready-to-use campaigns including Home Buyer Nurture (single property marketing) and Home Buyers List (multiple property listings by area or price range)
- **Online Advertising Integration**: Templates and tools for digital advertising campaigns
- **Social Media Management**: Pre-built social media posts and content templates
- **Mobile Responsive Design**: Fully optimized for mobile devices to capture leads from all platforms

## Use Cases
- **New Agent Onboarding**: Quick setup for new real estate agents joining a brokerage, providing immediate access to professional marketing tools
- **Property Marketing Campaigns**: Launch targeted campaigns for single listings or property portfolios with pre-built funnels and automated follow-up sequences
- **Lead Nurturing**: Automated email and SMS sequences guide prospects through each stage of the buying or selling journey
- **Client Appointment Management**: Streamline scheduling for property viewings, consultations, and closings with integrated calendar systems
