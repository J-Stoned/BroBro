# Gym Snapshot

## Marketplace
Official Marketplace

## Description
The Gym Snapshot serves as a foundational template designed for establishing sub-accounts specifically for fitness facilities and gym businesses. It provides a comprehensive toolkit to capture, nurture, and close leads with specialized features tailored to the unique needs of gym operations, member management, and fitness service marketing.

## Key Features
- **Fitness Website Platform**: Pre-built website templates optimized for gyms with appealing designs and seamless workflows to attract fitness enthusiasts
- **Lead Capture System**: Customizable forms and surveys integrated with lead-capturing workflows specific to gym membership inquiries and fitness assessments
- **Service & Class Calendars**: Multiple calendar types including service-specific calendars for personal training, group classes, and consultations, plus automated class booking functionality
- **Automated Workflows**: Six pre-configured workflows designed for gym operations including member onboarding, retention campaigns, and lead nurturing sequences
- **Email & SMS Campaigns**: Customizable messaging campaigns with automated sending, follow-up sequences, and de-duplication of campaign recipients
- **Pipeline Management**: Typical sales pipeline for monitoring new membership opportunities and tracking lead progress through the conversion funnel
- **Online Advertising Tools**: Integrated capabilities for digital advertising campaigns targeting local fitness markets
- **Member Retention Features**: Automated systems for review management, retention campaigns, and dynamic content personalization
- **CRM Integration**: Powerful automation that enables automatic lead movement and trigger actions based on specific pipeline stages

## Use Cases
- **New Gym Launch**: Complete digital infrastructure setup for new fitness facilities, providing all necessary tools for member acquisition from day one
- **Membership Drive Campaigns**: Automated marketing campaigns with customizable landing pages and follow-up sequences to convert prospects into paying members
- **Class & Training Management**: Streamlined booking and scheduling for group fitness classes, personal training sessions, and specialized fitness programs
- **Member Retention Programs**: Automated engagement campaigns, review requests, and re-engagement sequences to reduce churn and improve member lifetime value
