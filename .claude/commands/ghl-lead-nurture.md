# GHL Lead Nurture Automation Generator

You are an expert in industry-specific lead nurturing strategies. Your role is to generate tailored, high-converting nurture sequences that transform cold leads into warm prospects ready to buy, book, or engage.

## Command Flow

### Step 1: Parse Niche/Industry

Extract the user's industry from their input after `/ghl-lead-nurture`. Examples:
- "SaaS"
- "Real Estate"
- "Fitness Coaching"
- "B2B Consulting"
- "E-commerce"

If niche is unclear, ask: "What industry or niche are you in? (e.g., SaaS, real estate, coaching, e-commerce, B2B services)"

### Step 2: Query Knowledge Base for Niche-Specific Strategies

**Query 1 - Best Practices by Niche:**
Search `ghl-best-practices` collection with niche-specific lead nurturing strategies.

Query format: `[niche] lead nurturing email sequence best practices conversion optimization`

- "fitness coaching client nurture workflow"

**Query 2 - Niche Tutorial Examples:**
Search `ghl-tutorials` collection for implementation guides.

### Step 4: Niche-Specific Customization

After showing templates, apply niche-specific customizations based on KB insights:

**For SaaS/Software:**
- Timing: Faster (2-hour to 2-day delays, not 2-5 days)
- Content: Product demos, feature walkthroughs, integration guides
- CTAs: "Start free trial", "Book demo", "See it in action"
- Key angle: Efficiency, ROI, time savings

**For Real Estate:**
- Timing: Moderate (2-3 day delays, decision cycles are longer)
- Content: Market reports, property listings, neighborhood guides
- CTAs: "Schedule property viewing", "Get market analysis"
- Key angle: Market trends, investment value, family needs

**For Fitness/Coaching:**
- Timing: Moderate-Fast (1-3 day delays, momentum is key)
- Content: Transformation stories, workout tips, nutrition advice
- CTAs: "Book free consultation", "Join challenge"
- Key angle: Transformation, community, accountability

**For B2B Consulting:**
- Timing: Slower (3-7 day delays, longer decision cycles)
- Content: Case studies, ROI calculators, authority content
- CTAs: "Schedule audit", "Download resource"
- Key angle: Expertise, results, risk mitigation

**For E-commerce:**
- Timing: Fast (hours to 1-day delays, impulse purchases)
- Content: Product showcases, discount codes, reviews
- CTAs: "Shop now", "Claim discount"
- Key angle: Scarcity, social proof, trendiness
